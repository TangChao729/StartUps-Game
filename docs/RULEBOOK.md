# StartUps — Rulebook

## Components
- A deck of cards (6 company types, each with multiple copies)
- 6 Anti-Monopoly chips (one per company)
- Money coins (two sides: **1** and **3**)
- **-1 point chips** for debt

## Player Count & Setup
- **3–6 players**
- Each player starts with **10 money** (all "1" coins)
- Deal each player **3 cards**
- Remove **5 cards** from the game face-down (unknown to all)
- Remaining cards form the **draw deck** in the center
- **Market** starts empty (cards placed here during play)

---

## Turn Structure
On your turn, choose **one of two actions**:

### Action A: Draw from Deck
1. **First**, place 1 coin on every card currently in the Market — **unless** you hold that card's Anti-Monopoly chip
2. Draw a card (you now have 4 cards in hand)
3. Then choose one of:
   - **Play** one card face-up in front of you (your tableau)
   - **Place** one card face-up into the Market

### Action B: Take from Market
1. **Cannot** take a card if you hold that card's Anti-Monopoly chip
2. Take the card + collect any coins on it
3. Then choose one of:
   - **Play** one card face-up in front of you
   - **Place** one card face-up into the Market
   - **Cannot** return a card of the same type as the card you just took

---

## Anti-Monopoly Chips
- When you play cards into your tableau such that you now have **strictly the most** of a given company, you take that company's Anti-Monopoly chip (even stealing it from another player)
- Holding a chip means:
  - You **don't pay coins** when drawing from the deck for that company's Market card
  - You **can't take** that company's card from the Market

---

## End of Round — Game End Trigger
- Game ends when the **last card is drawn from the deck**
- All players add their remaining **hand cards** to their tableau

---

## Scoring Phase
Go through each company (starting with **Giraffe Beer**):

1. Find the player with the **most cards** of that company in their tableau
2. All **other players** pay that player **1 money per card they hold** of that company
3. **Tie = nobody gets paid** for that company

### Coin Flip Rule
- Coins paid to another player are flipped to their **"3" side**
- Received coins are worth **3 points** at end of game
- You pay using your **"1" coins only** — you cannot give "3" coins to others
- If you run out of "1" coins: the recipient takes a **"3" coin from the bank** instead
- If you can't pay at all: take a **"-1" chip** (worth -1 point at end)

---

## Final Score
- Count all money (1-side coins = 1pt, 3-side coins = 3pt)
- Subtract any -1 chips
- **Highest total wins**

---

## Key Rules — Edge Cases
| Situation | Rule |
|---|---|
| Tie for most of a company | Nobody gets paid |
| Taking same company card back to Market | Not allowed |
| Anti-Monopoly chip holder drawing | Skips placing coin on that company's Market card |
| Anti-Monopoly chip holder taking | Cannot take that company's Market card |
| Paying with no "1" coins left | Recipient takes "3" coin from bank |
| Completely broke when paying | Take -1 chip |
